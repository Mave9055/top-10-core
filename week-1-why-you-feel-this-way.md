## WEEK 1: WHY YOU FEEL THIS WAY
**Laws Covered:** Law 1 (The Alarm) & Law 2 (The Ghosts)  
**Time:** 120 minutes  

---

### [0:00 – 10:00] THE ANCHOR
Listen up. Yellow light. Red light. You run this room. If you need to leave, you leave. If you need to stand, you stand. You are in charge.

Feet flat on the floor. Hand on chest. Hand on belly. Breathe in for four. Hold. Out for six. Do it again. Good.

Last session was the deal: you own your story, we look at the map. Today we dive into the first two laws of your wiring.

---

### [10:00 – 40:00] LAW 1: THE ALARM
**Law:** Your alarm never shuts off.  
**Tool:** *"My alarm is going off. That's not weakness. That's my body doing its job."*

You know the feeling. You're sitting in a quiet room. Nothing's wrong. But your heart is pounding. Your jaw is tight. You're scanning for exits. That's not weakness. That's wiring.

**The Episode:**
> *"For years, I thought I was broken... Then a therapist looked me in the eye and said, 'It's not what's wrong with you. It's what happened to you.' Complex PTSD. It clicked."*

> *"They said I was born early... Three pounds, four ounces of undercooked soul dropped into a box of glass and wires... My body learned distance before my mind even knew the word."*

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Early Danger**: The body learns danger is normal.
2. **Blueprint Installed**: "Don't cry. Don't take space. Stay still."
3. **Alarm Soldered to ON**: Threat detector set to max.
4. **Shame Hits**: "Why can't I just relax?"

---

### [40:00 – 70:00] LAW 2: THE GHOSTS
**Law:** You're carrying ghosts you never met.  
**Tool:** *"That's not mine. That's a ghost."*

Some of what you feel isn't even yours. It was passed down. Their trauma wrote itself into your nervous system before you took your first breath.

**The Episode:**
> *"The living room filled with paramedics. 'Who am I?' my sister asked. 'Paula,' Mom said – naming her dead sister."*

Twenty-three names. Twenty-three people who died. Their loss lived in his body. You didn't just inherit your parents' eye color. You inherited their alarm settings.

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Inheritance**: You come out already wired for a war you never fought.
2. **Pattern Recognition**: Expecting tragedy isn't negative; it's data-driven prediction based on family history.
3. **The Tab**: The body keeps score of every loss handed down like an heirloom.

---

### [70:00 – 90:00] THE COMBINED MIRROR (Silent)
Sit. Breathe. Notice.
- If you've ever been called "too sensitive."
- If you've ever felt anxious when nothing was wrong.
- If you see the same patterns in your family – the same fights, the same addiction.
- If you feel like you were born tired.

That's not your fault. You didn't start the fire. You just got handed the match.
