## SESSION 00: THE SUMMONS (Recruitment Briefing for 5-Week Top 10 Core)

**Time:** 25 minutes (live pitch or video)  
**Purpose:** Make them want the 5-week Top 10 Core so badly they clear their calendar.  
**Voice:** Sergeant. Peer. No bullshit.  
**Format:** Word-for-word script. No facilitator notes.

---

### [0:00 – 2:00] THE OPENING

Listen up.

I'm not here to ask how you feel. I'm not here to hold your hand.

I'm here to tell you why your brain feels like a fire alarm that won't shut off – and what you can actually do about it.

Most programs ask: "What's wrong with you?"

We don't care.

Most programs want your story.

We don't want it.

Most programs try to fix your feelings.

We're here to look at your wiring.

---

### [2:00 – 5:00] THE SOVEREIGNTY CLAUSE

Here's the deal. You own your story. You will never be asked to share a single detail of your past. Not one.

We are not here to dig through your trauma. We are not here to make you cry. We are here to look at the map – not your scars.

You can sit through every session and never say a word about what happened to you. I don't need to know. Nobody here needs to know.

We are mechanics. We look at the machine. We don't need your life story to fix the wiring.

That's the deal. You keep your story. We give you the map.

---

### [5:00 – 10:00] THE RESCUE BOAT – WHY YOU'RE NOT WEAK

Now let's talk about the thing you're most ashamed of.

The bottle. The pipe. The pill. The rage. The isolation. The thing you reach for when the noise in your head won't stop.

Here's what they never told you.

**When that alarm won't shut off, you reach for anything that dials it down long enough to breathe and stay alive another night. That's the rescue boat.**

Society loves to blame the boat. "You're just an addict. Weak. Selfish. Why can't you just stop?"

They never ask why the boat launched in the first place. They don't want to look at the storm that made the water so rough you needed anything to keep from drowning.

**The rescue boat is not the enemy. The rescue boat is proof you still wanted to live.**

Every time you picked up, every time you used, every time you reached for something to turn down the noise – that wasn't weakness. That was your nervous system screaming: "I want to live. I just don't know how anymore."

This course doesn't tell you to stop using. That's between you and whatever you believe in.

**This course shows you why you started.** And why stopping has been so hard. And builds you other tools so the boat becomes less necessary.

---

### [10:00 – 18:00] THE TOP 10 CORE – WHAT YOU'LL LEARN IN 5 WEEKS

We're going to run a **5-week accelerated core**. One session per week, covering two laws each. Ten laws total.

Here's exactly what you'll learn:

**Week 1 – Why You Feel This Way**
Laws 1 & 2: The Alarm and The Ghosts. Why you can't relax and why you carry weight you didn't earn.

**Week 2 – Why You Get Stuck**
Laws 3 & 9: The Shelter and The Freeze. Why you stay in bad patterns and why you lock up when it matters.

**Week 3 – Anger and Vigilance**
Laws 4 & 8: The Mourning and The Exit. Why rage is a funeral and why you never feel safe.

**Week 4 – How You Act**
Laws 5 & 7: The War-Work and The Disappearing. Why overwork is panic and why you erase yourself.

**Week 5 – Who You Are**
Laws 6 & 10: The Mirror and The Circuit Breaker. Why you think you're a monster and why you leave your body.

That's the core. Ten laws. Five weeks.

If you finish those five weeks and you think this is shit, you walk away and we never call you.

But if you finish and you realize you finally have the manual for your machine... the rest of the map – the other 66 sessions – is waiting.

You don't have to sign up for a year. You just have to sign up for Week One.

### [18:00 – 22:00] THE STAKES – WHY THIS MATTERS

Seventeen point five veterans a day die by suicide. Sixty-one percent of them had no recent VA contact.

Not because they didn't need help. Because the system that was supposed to help them triggered the exact survival responses they were trying to get help for.

The legal system measures compliance. Trauma measures survival. They speak different languages.

Your family tells you to suck it up. Be a man. Get over it.

You've been gaslit. Not by one person. By the whole damn system.

**This course is the revolution.** Not politics. Not protests. Just understanding. Just naming. Just refusing to carry the shame anymore.

When the judge calls you noncompliant, you'll say: "That was freeze. My body was trying to survive."

When the doctor dismisses you, you'll say: "I have a trauma injury. Here's what I need."

When your family tells you to man up, you'll say: "I'm not weak. I've been carrying more than you'll ever know."

That's the revolution. And it starts here.
