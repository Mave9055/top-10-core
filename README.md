# Top 10 Core: The Summons

![Top 10 Core Poster](top-10-core-poster.png)

This repository contains the recruitment briefing and session scripts for the **5-Week Top 10 Core** program.

## Overview

The program is designed as a 5-week accelerated core covering ten laws that address trauma, survival mechanisms, and personal sovereignty. Each week consists of one comprehensive session covering two laws.

| Week | Theme | Session Script | Laws Covered |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Week 1** | Why You Feel This Way | [Week 1 Script](week-1-why-you-feel-this-way.md) | Law 1 (The Alarm), Law 2 (The Ghosts) |
| **Week 2** | Why You Get Stuck | [Week 2 Script](week-2-why-you-get-stuck.md) | Law 3 (The Shelter), Law 9 (The Freeze) |
| **Week 3** | Anger and Vigilance | [Week 3 Script](week-3-anger-and-vigilance.md) | Law 4 (The Mourning), Law 8 (The Exit) |
| **Week 4** | How You Act | [Week 4 Script](week-4-how-you-act.md) | Law 5 (The War-Work), Law 7 (The Disappearing) |
| **Week 5** | Who You Are | [Week 5 Script](week-5-who-you-are.md) | Law 6 (The Mirror), Law 10 (The Circuit Breaker) |
