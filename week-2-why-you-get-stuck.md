## WEEK 2: WHY YOU GET STUCK
**Laws Covered:** Law 3 (The Shelter) & Law 9 (The Freeze)  
**Time:** 120 minutes  

---

### [0:00 – 10:00] THE ANCHOR
Feet flat. Hand on chest. Breathe in for four. Hold. Out for six.

Last week: The Alarm and The Ghosts. You learned why you're wired for danger. Today: Why that wiring keeps you stuck in places and patterns that hurt.

---

### [10:00 – 40:00] LAW 3: THE SHELTER
**Law:** A dangerous shelter is still a shelter.  
**Tool:** *"My system is looking for a dangerous shelter."*

You've stayed in jobs that burned you out. Relationships that hurt you. Not because you're weak. Because your nervous system learned: *Being alone is death. Attachment is survival – even if it hurts.*

**The Episode:**
> *"Twenty years, four kids, and a obliterated marriage later... I tried to leave. I always came back. For them. Never for her."*
> *"Hiding in closets – literally becoming invisible... My existence felt like something that needed to be hidden."*

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Attachment = Survival**: The brain doesn't distinguish between good and bad attachment, only *presence* vs *absence*.
2. **The Trauma Bond**: Choosing the familiar fire over the unknown cold.

---

### [40:00 – 70:00] LAW 9: THE FREEZE
**Law:** You stop when you can't fight or run.  
**Tool:** *"That's freeze. My system is trying to survive."*

A judge asks a question, and your mind goes blank. A fight happens, and you watch from behind glass. That's not "noncompliance." That's freeze.

**The Episode:**
> *"I was six, huddled under a thin blanket... fear was a vise – cold, tight, locking me in place... If I moved, I'd be next."*
> *"Mom was in the living room... I froze. Not metaphorically. Physically. Exactly like I had under that blanket as a kid."*

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Biology Hits the Brakes**: When threat is too big to fight or flee, the brainstem shuts down movement.
2. **The System Punishes Freeze**: The legal system measures compliance; trauma measures survival. They speak different languages.
