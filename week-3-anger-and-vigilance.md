## WEEK 3: ANGER AND VIGILANCE
**Laws Covered:** Law 4 (The Mourning) & Law 8 (The Exit)  
**Time:** 120 minutes  

---

### [0:00 – 10:00] THE ANCHOR
Feet flat. Hand on chest. Breathe. 

Last week: The Shelter and The Freeze. Today: Why you're so angry, and why you can never just relax.

---

### [10:00 – 40:00] LAW 4: THE MOURNING
**Law:** What you couldn't mourn haunts you.  
**Tool:** *"This isn't rage. This is a funeral I never had."*

Anger is just grief with teeth. It's the bodyguard for the pain you weren't allowed to feel.

**The Episode:**
> *"Twenty-three names... some he never even met. But their loss lived in his body... Every funeral he couldn't attend."*
> *"Grief finally crushed me... 'Shut up,' she said. 'I have work tomorrow.' Something inside me broke that night."*

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Grief Compresses**: When you're in survival mode, you don't get to mourn. The grief packs down like a spring.
2. **Rage = Overflow**: The spring snaps. The rage feels active and powerful, hiding the crushing weight of loss.

---

### [40:00 – 70:00] LAW 8: THE EXIT
**Law:** You scan every room for exits.  
**Tool:** *"That's my threat detector. It's doing its job. There's no predator here."*

You can't sit with your back to the door. You jump at loud noises. That's not paranoia. That's hypervigilance.

**The Episode:**
> *"A car would backfire... in half a second, his heart was at 140 BPM... scanning for the exit. He felt ashamed."*
> *"The threat detector stays set to sensitive, always ready to blare. Sleep gets broken... the body never fully relaxes."*

**The Mechanism:**
1. **Threat Detector Set to 'MAX'**: Your brainstem recalibrates to scan for threat 24/7 because it kept you alive once.
2. **The Exhaustion Loop**: Living like this is exhausting. Shame makes the alarm worse.

---
